Terms & Conditions

Please review these terms carefully before enrolling. By enrolling, you agree to these conditions.

1. Course Structure & Tuition

Program Details

  • Each course consists of 20 live sessions over 10 weeks
  • Two 60-minute Zoom sessions per week
  • Minimum 5 learners required for a cohort to run
  • Maximum 12 learners per cohort
  • All materials provided via Google Classroom

Tuition

  • Standard tuition: $1,200 per 10-week course
  • Effective rate: $60/hour for small-group instruction
  • Payment is due in full before the first session to secure your seat

2. Referral Discount Program

  • Discount: $75 off for both you and your referred friend
  • Eligibility: One referral discount per enrollment
  • Both parties must enroll in the same term for the discount to apply
  • Referral must be mentioned at time of enrollment
  • Cannot be combined with other promotional offers
  • Referred friend must be a new student (first-time enrollment)

3. Refund & Cancellation Policy

Student-Initiated Withdrawals

  • Before course starts: Full refund minus $50 administrative fee
  • Before week 2: 50% refund of tuition
  • After week 2: No refunds available
  • All refund requests must be submitted in writing to montanarowl@gmail.com

School-Initiated Cancellations

  • If a cohort doesn't meet the minimum enrollment (5 learners), you'll receive a full refund or option to transfer to another cohort
  • If we cancel a course mid-term, you'll receive a prorated refund for remaining sessions
  • We reserve the right to cancel courses due to unforeseen circumstances

Missed Classes

  • Recordings and materials available for missed sessions via Google Classroom
  • Office hours available for catch-up support
  • No refunds or credits for individual missed classes

4. Student Expectations

Attendance & Participation

  • Arrive on time for scheduled sessions
  • Actively participate in class activities and discussions
  • Complete assigned homework and practice activities
  • Communicate absences in advance when possible

Behavior & Respect

  • Treat instructors and fellow learners with respect
  • Maintain a supportive and inclusive learning environment
  • Follow instructor guidance and classroom norms
  • Disruptive behavior may result in dismissal without refund

Technical Requirements

  • Stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps download/upload)
  • Computer or tablet with webcam and microphone
  • Zoom application installed and tested before first class
  • Access to Google Classroom for materials
  • Quiet learning environment free from distractions

5. Intellectual Property

  • All course materials, recordings, and content are proprietary
  • Materials are for enrolled students' personal use only
  • Sharing, distributing, or reproducing materials is prohibited
  • Recording live sessions without permission is not allowed

6. Privacy & Data

  • We collect and store student information for course administration
  • Email addresses used for course communications and updates
  • We will not sell or share your information with third parties
  • Session recordings may be stored temporarily for student review
  • You may request deletion of your data after course completion

7. Limitation of Liability

  • We strive for excellent instruction but cannot guarantee specific outcomes
  • Language proficiency depends on individual effort and practice
  • We are not liable for technical issues beyond our control
  • Maximum liability limited to tuition paid for the course

8. Changes to Terms

  • We reserve the right to update these terms with notice to enrolled students
  • Significant changes will be communicated via email
  • Continued enrollment constitutes acceptance of updated terms
  • Check this page periodically for the most current version
